About TARANG 2017

Team up with musicians across genres irrespective of college to win a Recording Deal of 1.5Lakhs! Cover an existing piece, or create your own. Come as a band and the stage is yours.


Online Registration (Last Date – 18 th Oct)

  1. Submit a 3-minute long video showcasing your team’s practice routine/previous performances to events@bits-oasis.org and cc the same to pcr@bits-oasis.org.
  2. Use Subject “Band Name - Oasis ’17 Tarang Video Submission”.
  3. Band members can be from different colleges. Each member should have a valid student ID.
  4. Selected 15 bands shall be announced on 20th Oct on BITS Fb page.
  5. The selected teams, once on-campus should report to the Department of Theatre room (2214, FD-II) with their IDs post registration at the Controls booth.

Rules

    Round 1 (1st November)

  1. Bands can perform any songs of their choice - Cover or Original.

  2. Maximum of 10 members and minimum of 3 members per band.

  3. All members of the band need not be of the same college though each member should have a valid Student ID.

  4. Total Time Limit: 20 minutes (including sound-check).

  5. If a team exceeds the time allocated for sound check, the extra time taken shall be reduced from the performance time. An indicative announcement will be made at the time limit of 20 minutes to notify the judges about the extension.

  6. 5% of the total marks would be deducted for every extra minute. Sounds would be cut after 23 minutes.

  7. Use of Pre-Recorded music (e.g., sequences, keyboard samples, guitar/octapad loops) will result in disqualification.

  8. The songs should have lyrics from any Indian language. However, a refrain with English lyrics is allowed.

  9. Purely classical numbers are not allowed. However, a classical refrain is allowed.

  10. Bands should bring their own instruments. Drum kit and sounds equipment will be provided by BITS Pilani.

  11. Only the top three teams from this round can participate in the Equals Fusion Music Night round.


    Round 2 - Equals Fusion Music Night (3rd November)

    This event is to showcase that music has no language or boundaries. BITS Pilani is proud to debut a new platform where musicians from various genres and cultures would collaborate to produce new music! Imagine those magical instances when music was created when you first met your band members! EQUALS is an opportunity to collaborate with unique instruments and folk musicians of India and create something unique.

  1. The finalist teams will be allocated a practice room for a period of 48 hours to collaborate with the reputed folk artists from Jaisalmer provided by Anahad Foundation.

  2. Each team will be provided with a Drum-kit and required amps.

  3. The bands along with the folk artists have to prepare a 20-minute fusion performance which they will present in the Equals Fusion Music Night.

  4. The teams have the choice to only go ahead with few members from their original contingent for Tarang, as long as there are at least 3 band members contributing.

  5. The teams will be performing in the Main Auditorium of BITS Pilani and will be a part of the Equals Fusion Music Night (Prof Show).

    Judging Criteria

  1. Creativity and Originality

  2. Quality of Vocals and Instrumental Music

  3. Tightness and Synchronization

  4. Choice of Song or Composition

  5. Body Language and Overall Impact

PRIZES

Winner-

  1. Audio Recording Deal worth of 50K.

  2. Music Video Recording Deal worth of 30K.

  3. Professional Band Photoshoot worth of 20K.

  4. Worldwide Music Distribution to around 30 platforms including Spotify and iTunes.

  5. Music Business Coaching from alumni of Berklee College of Music.

  6. Cash Prize of 25K+ (subject to vary).

Runner Ups-

  1. Multi-Track Audio-Video recording of the Final performance.

  2. Music Business Coaching from alumni of Berklee College of Music.

  3. Cash Prize of 20K+.
